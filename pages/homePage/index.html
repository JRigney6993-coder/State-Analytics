What is SPA?


SPA (State Pircing Analytics) is a state based comparison, where you can compare different basic needs between states. This includes insurance rates, pricing, healthcare, and more! SPA is the perfect way to quickly see different useful information about each state.

How Do You Use SPA?


To use SPA, click on "search" found at the top of this page. This will bring you to a page where you can select two different states, and then compare them based on what you choose. For instance, you can compare Texas's and Wyoming's population, or New York's and Arizona's state revenue. .



You select the first state you want to compare on the left and then the second state you want to compare on the right. Then you will choose what you want to compare them by in the center of the page. Hit enter and you have your desired results!

What are our Goals?


The main goal for SPA is to help those in need. Familes who need to move across the country, people who are too poor to live in a certain state, and just those who need access to this information. It helps everyone, and SPA is for the common man. Anyone can use it, and that is what's important.
